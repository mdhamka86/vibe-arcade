# Staking Rules

Flat-staking, adopted 26 June 2026, replacing the earlier variable staking.

## The flat rules

- **Standard unit: $5.** Every stake is a whole number of $5 units. No other
  increments.
- **Med-High convergence spot:** $10 WIN + $5 PLA (= 2 lots WIN, 1 lot PLA).
- **Marginal spot:** $5 PLA-only (= 1 lot PLA).
- Lots = count of $5 units. WIN $10 = 2 lots. PLA $5 = 1 lot.

## Selection bias (26 June rule)

Bias the whole book toward Medium-High convergence. Trim marginal Medium /
Low-Med PLA insurance rather than padding the book with it. Fewer, better spots.

## Discipline

- Never chase losses.
- Stake by convergence band, never by emotion, never by confidence label.
- WIN-pool bets underperform PLA structurally — keep the WIN exposure to the
  Med-High tier only, per the rules above.

## The sizing-change gate (do NOT bump casually)

Before changing stake sizes at all:

- Wait for roughly **35-40 Medium-High settled lines still clearly green**.
- Only then test a **gentle bump (~$7-8)**, and track that experiment
  **separately** from the main flat-staked ledger.

Do not freelance stake sizes ahead of this gate. The whole edge rests on
disciplined flat staking through a big enough green sample first.
